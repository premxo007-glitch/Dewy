Our Story
From a vision
to a comeback.
2019
The Beginning
Dewy was founded with a vision to create high-quality, affordable beauty and skincare products that enhance natural glow and confidence — bringing a fresh, modern approach to everyday skincare, focusing on simplicity, hydration, and radiant results.
2020
The Challenge
The global COVID-19 pandemic disrupted operations and market growth. Supply chain issues, reduced consumer spending, and limited visibility caused the brand to lose momentum and gradually fade from the market.
2025
The Comeback
Determined to return, the founders rebuilt Dewy from the ground up — stronger identity, improved formulations, and a customer-focused approach. A premium yet accessible brand with a bright future ahead.
